ABOUT

Situated at the heart of the historical Matale, Arangala forest lodge is the secret heaven near the Arangala forest. This place is the ideal location for any travel lover to get lost in the breathtaking natural beauty around the Arangala forest and its surrounding area. No matter what your travel interest is the area around the Arangala forest Lodge matches to all since it is surrounded by natural beauties, historical places, and archaeological sites. Since the place is away from the hustle bustle of the city life this is the ideal location for you to spend the holiday in a more free and peaceful setting with your loved ones.
